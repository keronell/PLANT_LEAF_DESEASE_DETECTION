================================================================================
AUGMENTATION STRATEGIES SUMMARY
================================================================================

This document lists the augmentation strategies used in each experiment approach.

================================================================================
1. BASELINE AUGMENTATIONS (from 5_fine_tune_models.ipynb)
================================================================================

For PV (Plant Village) Domain Images:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Resize to (224x224)
• RandomHorizontalFlip (probability=0.5)
• Convert to Tensor
• Normalize (ImageNet mean/std)

For Field Domain Images:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Resize to (224x224)
• RandomHorizontalFlip (probability=0.5)
• ColorJitter:
  - brightness=0.3
  - contrast=0.3
• Convert to Tensor
• Normalize (ImageNet mean/std)

Note: PV images with poor field performance may also use "field-style" augmentation:
• Resize to (224x224)
• RandomHorizontalFlip (probability=0.5)
• RandomRotation (degrees=20)
• ColorJitter (brightness=0.4, contrast=0.4, saturation=0.3, hue=0.1)
• RandomAffine (translate=0.1, scale=0.8-1.2)
• Convert to Tensor
• RandomErasing (probability=0.3, scale=0.02-0.15)
• Normalize (ImageNet mean/std)


================================================================================
2. QUICK WINS AUGMENTATIONS (from 5.1_fine_tune_models_quick_wins.ipynb)
================================================================================

For PV (Plant Village) Domain Images:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Resize to (224x224)
• RandomHorizontalFlip (probability=0.5)
• Convert to Tensor
• Normalize (ImageNet mean/std)

For Field Domain Images (AGGRESSIVE):
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Resize to (224x224)
• RandomHorizontalFlip (probability=0.5)
• RandomVerticalFlip (probability=0.3) ⭐ NEW
• RandomRotation (degrees=30) ⭐ INCREASED from 20°
• ColorJitter: ⭐ ENHANCED
  - brightness=0.5 (increased from 0.3)
  - contrast=0.5 (increased from 0.3)
  - saturation=0.5 ⭐ NEW
  - hue=0.2 ⭐ NEW
• RandomAffine: ⭐ ENHANCED
  - translate=0.15 (increased from 0.1)
  - scale=0.7-1.3 (increased range from 0.8-1.2)
• RandomPerspective (distortion_scale=0.2, probability=0.3) ⭐ NEW
• GaussianBlur (kernel_size=3, sigma=0.1-0.5, probability=0.3) ⭐ NEW
• RandomAdjustSharpness (sharpness_factor=2, probability=0.3) ⭐ NEW
• Convert to Tensor
• RandomErasing: ⭐ ENHANCED
  - probability=0.4 (increased from 0.3)
  - scale=0.02-0.2 (increased from 0.02-0.15)
• Normalize (ImageNet mean/std)

Note: PV images with poor field performance may also use "field-style" augmentation
(same as baseline field-style).


================================================================================
3. PROGRESSIVE DOMAIN ADAPTATION AUGMENTATIONS 
   (from 5.2_progressive_domain_adaptation.ipynb)
================================================================================

For PV (Plant Village) Domain Images:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Resize to (224x224)
• RandomHorizontalFlip (probability=0.5)
• Convert to Tensor
• Normalize (ImageNet mean/std)

For Field Domain Images (AGGRESSIVE):
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Same as Quick Wins aggressive field augmentation (see above)

Note: The key difference in Progressive Domain Adaptation is NOT the augmentation
strategy, but rather the SAMPLING strategy:
- Progressive field data ratio: 10% → 50% over training epochs
- Increased field sample weight: 5x (vs 3x in Quick Wins)


================================================================================
KEY DIFFERENCES SUMMARY
================================================================================

Baseline vs Quick Wins/Progressive:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Field Augmentation Intensity:
   - Baseline: Mild (only horizontal flip + basic color jitter)
   - Quick Wins/Progressive: Aggressive (11 augmentation steps)

2. New Augmentations in Quick Wins/Progressive:
   ✓ RandomVerticalFlip
   ✓ RandomPerspective
   ✓ GaussianBlur
   ✓ RandomAdjustSharpness
   ✓ Enhanced ColorJitter (with saturation and hue)
   ✓ Enhanced RandomErasing (higher probability and scale)

3. Enhanced Parameters:
   ✓ RandomRotation: 20° → 30°
   ✓ ColorJitter brightness/contrast: 0.3 → 0.5
   ✓ RandomAffine translate: 0.1 → 0.15
   ✓ RandomAffine scale range: 0.8-1.2 → 0.7-1.3

Quick Wins vs Progressive:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- Same augmentation strategies
- Different sampling strategies:
  * Quick Wins: Fixed 60% PV / 40% Field ratio, 3x field weight
  * Progressive: Dynamic 10% → 50% field ratio, 5x field weight


================================================================================
AUGMENTATION PARAMETERS REFERENCE
================================================================================

ImageNet Normalization:
  mean = [0.485, 0.456, 0.406]
  std = [0.229, 0.224, 0.225]

Image Size: 224x224

RandomHorizontalFlip: p=0.5 (50% chance)
RandomVerticalFlip: p=0.3 (30% chance)
RandomRotation: degrees=30 (for field images)
RandomPerspective: distortion_scale=0.2, p=0.3
GaussianBlur: kernel_size=3, sigma=(0.1, 0.5), p=0.3
RandomAdjustSharpness: sharpness_factor=2, p=0.3
RandomErasing: p=0.4 (field), scale=(0.02, 0.2), ratio=(0.3, 3.3)
